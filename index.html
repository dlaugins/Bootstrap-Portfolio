About Me

I am a certified 6-12 Math Teacher and have a masters degree in Industrial Engineering. At the beginning of my career I worked for Texas Instruments. I was repsonsible for keeping track of MLO costs for the products being built and working on ways to decrease the cost.

Connect with me

I left after 2 years to attend the Univeristy of Michigan and get my masters in Industrial Engineering specializing in Occupational Safety and Health. After graduating, I worked for Microswitch, a division of Honeywell, Tellabs, and Tadpole Technology.

After 10 years working in the engineering field, I became interested in teaching, and earned a 6-12 Math Teaching Certificate. I taught middle and high school in Austin, Texas and Sacramento California. I spent the last 14 years teaching middle school Math at Kealing Middle School.
